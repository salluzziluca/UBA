## Coordenadas esféricas
$$\LARGE(x,y,z)=(\rho.cos(\theta).sen(\phi), (\rho.sen(\theta).sen(\phi),(\rho.cos(\phi))$$
$$0<=\rho<=\infty$$
$$0<=\theta<=2 \pi$$
$$0<=\phi<=\pi$$
$$J(\rho,\phi,\theta)=\rho² sen(\phi)$$
