### Flip Flop J-K asincrónico 
| J   | K   | Q   |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 0   | 0   | Q   |
| 0   | 1   | 0   |
| 1   | 0   | 1   |
| 1    | 1    |$\bar{Q}$     |
0 -> 1 
11 ->1
10 -> 1
