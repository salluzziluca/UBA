 Teorema: Para todo x = (x_1, x_2, ..., x_n) \in R^n se tendrá que 
 $$X^{-1}((-\infty, x_{1}).(-\infty, x_{2}). \ \dots \ . (-\infty, x_{n})) \in Algebra$$

$F_{\mathbb{X}}(x)= P(X_{1}\leq x_{1}, \dots, X_{n}\leq x_{n})$
